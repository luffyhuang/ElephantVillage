Wild Elephant Tea

-The story behind "Wild Elephant Tea":-

-China House created the product "Wild Elephant Tea".-

———🍵———


This Pu'er tea comes from the tea gardens of local villagers


who are willing to live in harmony with elephants.


When consumers purchase the tea,


producers would benefit from the tea sales.


Thus, they may have better attitudes toward the elephants.


————————


———🍵———


On the other hand, all the profits generated from the "Wild Elephant Tea"


will be used for the installation of elephant-deterrent lights and


the development of more products to help local sustainable development.


Through "Wild Elephant Tea",


we hope to help more people coexist harmoniously with wild animals.


————————
