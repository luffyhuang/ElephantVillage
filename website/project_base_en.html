Project Base

In 2021, China House established a project base in Dashujiao Village.

By leading Chinese youth to Dashujiao Village for research and sustainable development projects, China House has alleviated the human-elephant conflict here to some extent.

Through the project base in Dashujiao Village, on the one hand, more young people can learn about wildlife conservation. On the other hand, practices of sustainable development projects can help to alleviate the local conflicts between humans and elephants. What's more, the establishment of the project base also can promote local tourism and increase the incomes of some local residents.
