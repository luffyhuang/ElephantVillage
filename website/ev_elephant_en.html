Elephants in Dashujiao Village

Follow us,and understand the life of elephants.


In 2011, Asian elephants first entered Zhengdong Town, Jiangcheng County.


After that, the elephants followed the Manlao River all the way north, passing through Manke Old Village and Man Laojie Village, and arrived at Dashujiao Village in 2017. For several years, these elephants have been roaming around the three villages. According to some residents, there are now more than 40 elephants wandering around Dashujiao Village for about 8 months per year.





Elephant monitors in Dashujiao Village post daily real-time location of the Asian Elephants on the Early Warning Platform in Jiangcheng County.
