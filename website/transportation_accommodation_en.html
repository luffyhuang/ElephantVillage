Transportation and Accommodation

There is only one hotel in Dashujiao village, with seven rooms divided into standard rooms and triple rooms.

Address: Group 2, Dashujiao Village, Kangping Town, Jiangcheng County, Pu 'er City

Contact Information: Wang Shaobin 15008706191

Dashujiao Village is located in Hani and Yi Autonomous County, Jiangcheng City, Yunnan Province, 65 kilometers away from Pu 'er City.


It takes about 2 hours and 10 minutes to drive from Pu 'er Airport to Dashujiao Village, and costs about 300 RMB by taxi. Driving route: county road 165 - yushui road - chayuan road -227 national road - Z002-j44 county road.
