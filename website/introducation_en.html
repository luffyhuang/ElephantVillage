Introduction to Dashujiao Village

Dashujiao Village is located in Kangping Town, Jiangcheng County, Pu'er City, Yunnan Province. There are 14 villagers groups with 2062 people.

The villagers are mainly Han, as well as ethnic minorities such as Hani and Dai. Most of them make a living from planting and animal husbandry. In March 2021, Dashujiao Village was included in the 2020 Yunnan Provincial Health Village List.
