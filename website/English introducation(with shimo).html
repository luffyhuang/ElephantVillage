Introduction of Dashujiao Village

      Dashujiao Village is located in Shuikoushan Town, Lingling District, Yongzhou City, Hunan Province. Before liberation, there was a big old tree in the village, which was built under the tree, so it was named Dashujiao. Later the big tree was destroyed by lightning, but the village name was still used to date. The village committee is based in Dashujiao and governs 22 villager groups including Dashujiao and Erbangtou.

image: The location of Dashujiao Village

      Dashujiao Village is located in Kangping Town, Jiangcheng County, Pu'er City, Yunnan Province. There are 14 villagers groups with 2062 people. The villagers are mainly Han, as well as ethnic minorities such as Hani and Dai. Most of them make a living from planting and animal husbandry.

image: The view of Dashujiao Village

      Dashujiao Village has Mengliehu Wetland Park, Hujiupo Tea Horse Ancient Road, Jiangcheng Niuluohe Tea Garden, Jiangcheng No. 7 Laos Boundary Stele, Yiguangsanguo and other tourist attractions. There are also Pu 'er Tobacco Leaf, Yao Dazongba, Beef Pickled Vegetable, Glutinous Rice Ssausage, and Saya Red Fruit.
