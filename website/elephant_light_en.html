Elephant-Deterrent Lights

In 2021, China House plans to install elephant-deterrent lights for 100 households "visited" by wild elephants most frequently.

Elephant-deterrent lights can alleviate the conflict between humans and elephants to a certain extent. The elephant-deterrent lights we designed have two functions——flickering and illuminating.






Elephant-deterrent lights could absorb solar energy during the day and give off flickering lights at night. This will keep the wild elephants away from the residential area, thus further helping villagers protect themselves from wild elephants and reduce the casualties caused by some elephants.



Besides, due to the lack of street lights, some villagers might be out of sight at night and suffer injuries from sudden "encounters" with the elephants. While with our lights, they may turn it into the lighting mode when needed, effectively avoiding conflicts at night.
